n°02 — the big one

        
BEST EMBEDDED
SYSTEM DESIGN 🏆

        
Ren-Energy — Sultan, Sanchez & Ulbenario · CCIS InnoVision 2026 · June 20, 2026


        

            

            

                
a reflection

                

                    Receiving the Best Embedded System Design award at CCIS InnoVision 2026 was an
                    amazing opportunity to showcase the months of hard work our team poured into
                    Ren-Energy. What made the experience truly worth it, though, wasn't the award
                    itself — it was the people. Walking guests through our system, answering their
                    questions, and watching ideas spark in real time reminded me why I build in the
                    first place. Technology becomes meaningful the moment it's shared. This
                    achievement taught me that innovation is never a solo effort; it grows through
                    collaboration, curiosity, and conversation. It deepened my motivation to keep
                    creating solutions that, in my own way, contribute to the world's innovations.
